Here is the ingredients used to cook the Eggless Truffle Cake:- 

For the sponge:

1. 150 ml Oil
2. 275 gram Sugar
3. 185 gram Milk maid
4. 375 gram Curd
5. 375 Flour
6. 9 gram Baking soda
7. 9 gram Baking powder

For the sugar syrup:

8. 200 gram Sugar
9. 200 ml Water

For truffle:

10. 500 gram Dark chocolate
11. 250 gram Fresh cream
